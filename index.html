I am a storyteller.


I am a master's of marketing student at Vanderbilt University, where I am developing the strategic framework and arsenal of tools to create messaging that resonates with people. Before deciding to pursue a graduate degree, I attended The University of the South, graduating summa cum laude with a BA in environmental arts and humanities. During my undergraduate studies, I realized the power of stories in the fight for environmental and social justice. My desire to learn how to craft impactful stories led me to Circa Design, a boutique branding agency where I gained expertise in adobe creative suite and applied visual storytelling to create and strengthen brands for small businesses.
